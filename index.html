My scratch games

Scratch is a block-programming language, and may appear at first limiting. But it's its limitation that encourage the scratch developper to create complex projects with only some math and basic rendering techniques.

I made several impressive projects that got rewarded by the scratch community, such as a fully-working 3D model Editor, playable on the browser, and huge MMO multiplayer games.
